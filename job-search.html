Job Search Prompts

Powerful prompts to help you land your dream job.

Resume Builder Prompt

Prompt: "I need a professional resume for a [Job Title] position. My experience includes [mention key responsibilities/achievements]. I have skills in [list skills]. My highest degree is [Degree] from [University]."

Cover Letter Generator Prompt

Prompt: "Write a compelling cover letter for a [Company Name] for the [Job Title] role. Highlight my experience in [specific experience] and my passion for [company's mission/industry]."

Interview Prep Q&A Prompt

Prompt: "Generate common interview questions for a [Job Title] role and provide concise, impactful answers for each, focusing on my [mention your strengths/experiences]."
